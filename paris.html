Discover the Magic of Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is globally renowned for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. The city's 19th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by wide boulevards and the River Seine. Beyond such landmarks as the Eiffel Tower and the 12th-century, Gothic Notre-Dame cathedral, the city is known for its cafe culture and designer boutiques along the Rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré.

From the iconic Eiffel Tower to the world-famous Louvre Museum, Paris offers an unparalleled blend of history, art, and romance. Stroll along the Seine River, explore charming neighborhoods like Montmartre, and indulge in world-class cuisine at sidewalk cafés.

Travel Tips for Paris

Transportation

Use the efficient Metro system to get around the city. Buy a weekly Navigo pass for unlimited travel on all public transport.

Dining

Make reservations in advance for popular restaurants. Don't forget to try local specialties like croissants, macarons, and French onion soup.

Money

Most places accept credit cards, but carry some cash for small purchases. Tipping is not mandatory but appreciated.

Language

Learn basic French phrases. Parisians appreciate the effort, even if you make mistakes. "Bonjour" and "Merci" go a long way.
